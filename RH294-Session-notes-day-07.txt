- Ansible Automation Platform (AAP 2.x)
- AAP components:-
  ansible-core
  ansible-navigator 
  ansible-execution environment 
  ansible-builder 
  ansible-controller 
  
- AAP's configuation files:-
  - ansible-navigator.yaml

- handlers, task control.

Playbook:-

- Configuration changes (apache)
- Application deployment (index.html).

- validate the work/application.
